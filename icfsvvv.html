The Indore Cancer Foundation is a not-for-profit, charitable organisation working in the field of cancer in central India. It was set up in December 1988 by Dr. Digpal Dharkar. The Foundation, a charitable trust registered under the Madhya Pradesh Public Trust Act, 1951, was settled on November 14th, 1989, by Smt. Usha Devi Holkar, ex-ruler of Indore, and her husband Mr. Satish Chandra Malhotra, Chairman, Empire Industries, Mumbai.
