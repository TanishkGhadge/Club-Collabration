Riyaaz: The Musical Club

Our club is a Family of students who bring their unique musical talents and interests to the table. From singers and instrumentalists to songwriters and those interested in music production, there's a place for everyone here. We regularly host jam sessions where you can collaborate with others, explore new genres, and hone your skills in a relaxed, supportive environment.

FAQ

What is Riyaaz

We are Riyaaz, the official registered music club of SVVV. A club, where passion for music unites us all! Our club is the perfect place where we connect, create, and share our love for music.

How can I join the club?

You can join by filling out the membership form during recruitment or contact any of the club members.
